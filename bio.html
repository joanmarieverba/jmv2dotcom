 1950s 
Born in 1953 in the Boston, Massachusetts area. My father was an active duty Air Force officer at the time.
The family moved to Minnesota in 1958, after Dad's enlistment was over. We started out in an apartment in Minneapolis. (I called the apartment building a "little castle." It was a brick building.)
Not long after that, we moved to Minnetonka, a western suburb of Minneapolis. I had started kindergarten in Minneapolis at age 4, and transferred to Clear Springs Elementary in Minnetonka after the move.
